Phenylalanine
An essential amino acid. The human body cannot make it so it is essential to the diet. Phenylalanine that is ingested is largely transformed to form the amino acid tyrosine, which is used in protein synthesis. Too little phenylalanine curbs physical and intellectual growth. Phenylanine was first isolated in 1879. *People who have the genetic disorder phenylketonuria (PKU) or certain other health conditions, phenylalanine can be a serious health concern. Consult your doctor before!
